How It Works

Upload any photograph. The app takes every single pixel from your image and rearranges them to recreate a completely different picture.

No pixels are added or removed — the set is identical. Only their positions change. You watch each pixel migrate from its original location to its new home in real time.

The result is built entirely from your photo’s colours. Same pixels. Different truth.
